About Nykaa Legacy..🎉

Nykaa is India's premier omnichannel lifestyle retailer. Founded in 2012 by Falguni Nayar, it revolutionized the market by guaranteeing 100% authentic beauty and wellness products through direct brand procurement. Today, it serves over 52 million customers across its digital platforms and 276+ physical stores, offering over 4,000 global and local brands.
